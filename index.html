But at the SAME TIME, a huge amount of waste happens...

The waste is NOT because students are careless ‚Äî it's because the university lifestyle forces waste:

  • Students constantly move accommodations and they have to do instantaneously
  • They can't carry large or heavy items home
  • They can't store items over summer
  • Many items are required for short-term use only
  • Leases end abruptly, so students must throw away items fast

This is not a student problem. It is a system problem.
